User feeds

You can view news stories from a range of different sources such as BBC, Sky Sports and Talksport.

Loading more stories

To load more news stories on the news feed, simply pull the down the page, which will refresh the stories and load 5 more articles.

Social feeds

You can view social media posts from Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram for your chosen team.

Refresh social feed

To refresh the social feed, pull down the page and wait until it loads.

Image feed

To change your social feed to an image-only feed, simply slide the 'Image feed' slider to on.

User feeds

You can view news stories submitted by other users, relating to your chosen team.

Submit a news story

To submit a news story, click the icon on the user feeds page. Then enter the information you wish, making sure to fill out all fields. Tap the submit button and you're done! Refresh the user feeds page to view your new post.

Choose your team

To select your team, open the menu, and go to the settings page. From there you can select which team you want to see news for. As well as selecting a team, you can select a filter which will be applied to the news feeds page, showing only stories relating to that filter.
