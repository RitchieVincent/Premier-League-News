How to use this app

There are two types of feeds available to you. You can view either news stories from a range of multiple different sources such as BBC, Sky Sports and Talksport. The other type of feed is social feeds. These display Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram posts from your selected team.

To select your team, open the menu, and go to the settings page. From there you can select which team you want to see news for. As well as selecting a team, you can select a filter which will be applied to the news feeds page, showing only stories relating to that filter.

Loading more stories

To load more news stories on the news feed, simply pull the down the page, which will refresh the stories and load 5 more articles.

Refresh social feed

To refresh the social feed, pull down the page and wait until it loads.

Image feed

To change your social feed to an image-only feed, simply slide the 'Image feed' slider to on.
